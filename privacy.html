Privacy Policy

ETM-Tax Agent Office [ETM-TAO], Inc. · Last updated: July 2026

We respect your privacy and handle personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. What we collect

  • Contact details you enter: name, email, mobile number.
  • The Google Drive folder link you share, and the documents inside it (e.g., your LoA and related BIR notices) — for the purpose of your appraisal.
  • Payment reference information when you pay (we do not collect or store card numbers).

2. How we use it

We use your information solely to prepare and deliver your Executive Summary and, if you opt in, to prepare a Statutory Demand for your own signature. We do not sell your data or use it for unrelated purposes.

3. Your documents & your Drive

We access only the folder you share, on a read-only basis, and only for your case. You own your data; your Google Drive remains the record. We do not retain copies beyond what is needed to complete your engagement.

4. Payments

Online payments are processed by our third-party payment provider (PayMongo). Your card or wallet details are handled by the provider under their own security and privacy terms; we receive only a payment confirmation and reference.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We share information only with service providers necessary to deliver the service (e.g., the payment processor) or where required by law.

6. Your rights

Under RA 10173 you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

Email taxspecialista@kataxpayer.com or visit Kataxpayer on Facebook.
