Refund & Cancellation Policy

ETM-Tax Agent Office [ETM-TAO], Inc. · Last updated: July 2026

The Executive Summary is a professional appraisal service priced at ₱5,600.00 (₱5,000 + 12% VAT).

Our policy: your fee is fully creditable toward a Statutory Demand if you choose to proceed to that next level. If you do not proceed, the fee is non-refundable once the appraisal has begun, because it pays for professional work already performed.

1. Before we begin

If you have paid but we have not yet started your appraisal, contact us and we will either begin promptly or arrange a refund of the amount paid.

2. Once the appraisal has begun

Because the Executive Summary is professional work delivered specifically for you, the fee is non-refundable once work has started. However, it remains fully creditable to a Statutory Demand should you engage that service.

3. Duplicate or erroneous charges

If you were charged more than once, or in error, we will refund the erroneous amount in full. Contact us with your Order No. and payment reference.

4. How to request

Email taxspecialista@kataxpayer.com (include your Order No. and payment reference) or message Kataxpayer on Facebook. Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method, typically within 5–10 banking days.
